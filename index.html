CREADORES

Gorillaz is a virtual English band created in 1998 by Damon Albarn and Jamie Hewlett. The band is composed of four fictional members: 2-D, Noodle, Murdoc Niccals, and Russel Hobbs. Most of their songs, along with their entire fictional universe, are presented through animated music videos, traditional animation, and computer-generated animation, interviews, and short animated films. In reality, Albarn is the only permanent member of the band. The producer and executive Remi Kabaka Jr. began collaborating with the group in 2016 after providing the voice of Russel Hobbs for several years and was named an official member alongside Albarn and Hewlett in 2019 in the Gorillaz documentary, "Gorillaz: Reject False Icons."
